BroBruhBrah Privacy Policy

Effective October 29, 2025

Support

Who We Are

BroBruhBrah is a mobile game developed by Eric So, who serves as the data controller for this policy. For privacy questions or support, contact Eric So at brobrehbrahbruh@gmail.com.

Information We Collect and Why

The game does not create user accounts or upload personal data. Everything remains on your device to power these features:

  • Gameplay progress and unlocks: Tracks the modes, levels, and content you have opened.
  • Preferences: Saves audio levels, voice selections, and haptic toggles so the experience matches your choices.
  • Gameplay statistics: Records performance data that drives in-app feedback and achievements.
  • Voice pack entitlements: Stores proof that optional add-on voice packs were purchased so you can use them offline.

No personal identifiers, analytics, or advertising data leave your device.

Local Storage Practices and Retention

BroBruhBrah uses React Native’s AsyncStorage to keep preferences, statistics, and purchase entitlements on your device. We do not operate remote databases or cloud sync for the game.

Data remains available locally until you remove it. You can delete specific entries through in-app reset controls (where available) or clear everything by uninstalling the app or removing its storage in your operating system settings.

In-App Purchases

Optional voice packs are sold through the Apple App Store or Google Play Store. BroBruhBrah never sees or stores your payment details. The stores confirm the purchase, and the app stores a local entitlement so you can access the content without an internet connection.

When you reinstall the app with the same store account, use the platform’s restore purchases feature to reactivate any voice packs.

Third-Party Services

The current version of BroBruhBrah does not use third-party analytics, advertising networks, or remote services. Earlier versions that relied on cloud features have been retired.

Children’s Privacy

BroBruhBrah is intended for a general audience. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13, and the game does not transmit data off the device. Parents or guardians can remove all locally stored data by uninstalling the app or clearing its storage.

Managing or Deleting Your Data

You control all data stored by BroBruhBrah. To manage it:

  • Adjust or reset settings from within the app when options are provided.
  • Clear the app’s storage through your device settings (for example, Settings > Apps > BroBruhBrah > Storage > Clear Data on Android).
  • Uninstall the app, which permanently deletes all locally stored information.

Because no server-side backups exist, data removed from your device cannot be restored unless you have your own backup.

Contact

Questions, privacy concerns, or support requests can be sent to brobrehbrahbruh@gmail.com. Please include “BroBruhBrah Privacy” in the subject line so we can respond quickly.
