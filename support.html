Bro Bruh Brah Support

This page provides help resources and contact details for Bro Bruh Brah players.

Privacy Policy

Contact Support

If you run into issues, have questions, or want to share feedback, reach out and we’ll get back within two business days.

Email: brobrehbrahbruh@gmail.com

Include the following in your message to help us resolve things quickly:

  • Your device model (for example, iPhone 15, iPad Pro)
  • The operating system version (for example, iOS 18.2)
  • The Bro Bruh Brah app version listed in Settings > About
  • A short description of the issue, bug, or request
  • Any screenshots or screen recordings that illustrate the problem

Email is our primary support channel today. If other contact options become available, we will update this page.

Quick Self-Help Tips

Try these steps for the most common issues before reaching out:

  • Force close and reopen the app to refresh your session.
  • Check the App Store for updates so you’re on the latest version.
  • Restart your device if the app freezes or crashes.
  • Reset gameplay preferences from within the app if available.
  • Reinstall the app to clear local data if everything else fails.

We appreciate any follow-up letting us know whether these tips worked for you.
